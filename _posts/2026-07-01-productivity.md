# Happiness
A lot of the people think that living a life that is easy and comfortable is better and much more suitable for humans, but this is not true. In the past months I started schedule and program my time and daily habits. It was not that much hard for me to start, thanks to the amazing and cool __Productive__ community. I'm not saying it wasn't hard, but for sure it was easier than multiple last times that I started to do something in my life without any goals or motivations (Also I'll talk about motivation later in this blog). I'm not far complete and done in the case of finding my best environment to be product but I see __incredible__ amount of progress in myself!.

Before meting this community, I didn't know who I am. Maybe this will be funny for you but the reality is that I was in a special situation of wondering around my thoughts to see who I really want to become and even if I know that, _How_ i want to achieve it. I even remember that I was asking myself what is the purpose of me in this life. All these questions were someone related to one thing, and that things was I __liked__ the process of begin __productive__ but I just couldn't understand how can i do that or what is the way to implement it in my life. This process becomes easier when I found out that there is a community of people who think exactly like me. They _study_, _progress_ hard to become who they want to be and what they want to do everyday. Then with that said, after wondering around different communities, I finally answered so many of my questions.

Happiness is not living inside your comfort zone, or maybe spending time and an easy task and _acting_ like your doing or learning it. It is not the process of over-thinking or over-fantasizing something. it is simply the process of actually __doing__ that thing and trying to achieve the desire results. This is simply a problem in the modern days of technology for those who are mostly __consumers__ of digital information, instead of begin creative and __creating__ them. What we should call an _ideal_ and _good_ life is a life full of new experiments and creativity, a life that you want to learn something new and try really hard for it, _no effort will go unrewarded_.

Then for me a happy life, is a life full of learning new stuff and spending times on things that the're actually matter and make us excited for the future.

# Valuable habits makes valuable life
I'll not spend so much time talking about this since there is a great and valuable book, called __Atomic Habits__ that can explain this properly. This concept is another thing that I learned from _James_ and his community. Doing __valuable__ stuff that you __like__ and __curious__ about is the most important and fun thing to do in life. Most of us, may have this idea of _Loving_ something but not doing that thing because it is hard or simply needs so effort to be achieved. Or sometimes we fantasize things at the level that we lose our sanity for actually doing that thing. All these things are the result of chaining bad habits and getting used to things that mostly doesn't add anything to our knowledge or answer any of our main questions. As long as we understand this concept, we can flip it over to _getting used to good and valuable habits_ to make the life more beautiful and fun.

Doing __Study__ and __Learning__ stuff is a magical activity for me. It is the most beautiful way that we can rise and grow our mind to become more and more mature about all the things around us, including our beautifully complex universe, Thus combining all the things that we learn and creating new technologies and creations, is the most enjoyable activity. These are the things _James_ and his community or any other __productive__ communities are trying to show-case.

Then simply we can say, being __productive__ everyday, is the most beautiful thing that we can do in our life.

# My Setup
Productivity in my opinion is the action of doing stuff that are __valueable__ and you __like__. personally i measure The amount of _value_ of a task by looking at the __long term outcome__ of it + how much I like to do that task.

Mostly I __prioritize__ my tasks whenever I want to do them, this makes the process much more focused and interesting since you accomplish important stuff sooner than other stuff below it.

I use __Google Calendar__ as my main scheduling calendar and __Obsidian__ as my note taking system. Obsidian is the most fun part of it since it is not just a note taking app system for me anymore, currently I define all of my tasks using [__TaskNote__](https://github.com/callumalpass/tasknotes) plugin inside it, the _Task View_ of this plugin is a great feature since I can group, sort or search all of my tasks (or TODOs) using it. I'll make a proper blog post about it later.

and at the end of the day, I use any __pomodor timer__ app available to do my tasks via a specific time structure. Since pomodoro timer technique is not just a simple timer mechanism and it actually let you have short breaks, i do my tasks more efficiently. Currently I'm using [DMS pomodoro plugin](https://github.com/AvengeMedia/dms-plugins) since it works perfectly and it has shell integrations, but before this I used to use __TaskNote__ plugin's pomodoro timer tool.

I use [Aio Launcher](https://aiolauncher.app/) on my android phone, since it is _minimal_ and fast for app navigation. Plus it has cool widgets written in _Lua_, means it is really dynamic for users need.

I use [Okular](https://okular.kde.org/) to read PDFs, research paper or even annotates my ideas into PDF files. It's a good and lightweight tool with great features that is cross-platform and just works.

For the operating system, I mostly use [Fedora Linux](https://fedoraproject.org/) but I'll switch to other ones when i need to use certain apps that doesn't work on Linux.
